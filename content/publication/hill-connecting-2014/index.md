publication: '*Psychology & Health*'
doi: 10.1080/08870446.2014.884221
abstract: |
  The present research examined the effects of the social environment on the onset of specific health
  ailments. Using data from the Health and Retirement Study, we examined participants' responses to
  social environment questions in 2006 as predictors of onset of different health conditions over
  the next four years.
